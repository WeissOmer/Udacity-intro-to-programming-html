Brown Bear

Bears care deeply about family members. They will risk their lives and even fight to the death in order to save a cub or sibling from dange

  • Scientific Name : Ursus arctos
  • Average Length: 1.4-2.8 Meters
  • Average Lifespan: 35 Years
  • Habitat: Mountain Woodlands

The brown bear is a large mammal species of the bear family. This bear is one of the biggest and most famous carnivores. It is common in the northern hemisphere: in Europe, northern America and part of Asia and is adapted to cold and temperate climates.
